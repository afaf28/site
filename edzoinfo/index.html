e-DZO

e-Dzo is a virtual coach able to collect information through a simple dialogue, respond to questions, find solutions to regularly encountered problems, make recommendations, train individuals.

e-Dzo is smart, intuitive and adapts to users. He integrates each team to capture, organise, store, spread and make the team’s knowledge widely available in your company. e-Dzo is an avatar of teams’ expertise.

He is powered by artificial intelligence, and becomes the team’s memory holder, responsible for mastering the team’s know-how and expertise to provide it easily and instantaneously to any employee who may need it.

Coach
Adaptive Training

e-Dzo acts as a personal coach to each individual, he is capable of training each employee and alerting him about an important event. e-Dzo will ensure knowledge and information are up to date, apprehended and memorized.
